Khepra Ptah was introduced to the Yoruba tradition in the 90s. He holds his Masters in Education from Goddard College. Khepra received his Warriors (Esu, Ochoosi, and Ogun) in 2002. In 2012 he received Osayin, a year later he was initiated into IFA in the Fayemi lineage. Khepra also has been attuned as a Reiki Master since 21 years old. He offers classes and lectures.
